What does it mean to be a Contributor?

Contributors are essential to the Chesapeake Community Connect mission. By creating an account, Contributors gain the ability to create and share forums and posts, as well as comment on other Contributors’ content. Contributors can share stories, projects and efforts that are building resilience across the Chesapeake region. As a Contributor, you can aid in providing knowledge to this community on environmental issues and concerns, as well as other updates. Contributors are encouraged to include a location, resource links, and a written description to help others understand and engage with their project. Every contribution helps this community expand the network of learning and action that keeps this platform thriving.
