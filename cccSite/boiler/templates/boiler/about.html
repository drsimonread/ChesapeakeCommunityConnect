Coastal Community Resilience Research Team

The Coastal Community Resilience research team partners with Chesapeake Community Connect to better understand the needs, strengths, and challenges of coastal communities. Their work informs the design of tools and resources on this site, ensuring that our efforts are grounded in community input and focused on building long-term resilience.

R&D, QA, and Capstone Project Teams

Our Research & Development, Quality Assurance, and Capstone project teams work together to bring Chesapeake Community Connect to life. R&D focuses on designing and building new features, QA ensures that everything functions reliably for our users, and Capstone students contribute real-world development effort as part of their academic experience. Together, these teams support our shared mission of connecting coastal communities with the resources they need.
