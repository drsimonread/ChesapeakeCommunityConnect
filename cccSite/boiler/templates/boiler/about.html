Research Team

Our research team focuses on coastal community resilience, working to understand and address environmental and societal challenges facing coastal regions. Through collaboration, data analysis, and community engagement, the team develops insights and solutions that support sustainable and resilient coastal communities.

R&D, QA, and Capstone Teams

The Software Startup Simulator Capstone brings together students in Research & Development, Quality Assurance, and project teams to collaborate on real-world software projects. The R&D team focuses on building and enhancing application features, while QA ensures quality through testing and documentation. Together, these teams simulate a professional development environment, delivering meaningful solutions for real clients.
