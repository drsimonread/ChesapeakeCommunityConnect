Research Team

The Research Team is responsible for analyzing community needs and identifying real-world problems that can be addressed through software solutions. They conduct background research, gather requirements, and ensure that development efforts align with user expectations and project goals.

Their work serves as the foundation for the entire project. By studying existing systems and consulting stakeholders, the research team helps define what features should be built and why they are important. This ensures the final product is both useful and relevant to the community it serves.

R&D / QA / Capstone Team

This combined group brings together Research & Development, Quality Assurance, and Capstone collaboration to design, build, and refine the final application. Each subgroup plays a key role in delivering a functional and polished product.

The R&D team focuses on implementing features and improving system functionality. QA ensures that all components are tested thoroughly, documenting bugs and verifying fixes. The Capstone coordination group oversees integration, ensuring that all parts of the project work together smoothly.

Together, these teams follow a structured development process similar to a real software company, preparing students for professional roles in software engineering and collaborative development environments.
