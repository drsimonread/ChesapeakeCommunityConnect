About us

The Software Startup Simulator capstone and the teams behind Chesapeake Community Connect.

Software Startup Simulator Capstone

The Software Startup Simulator Capstone is a unique opportunity for students in the St. Mary's College Computer Science Department to participate in a real-world development project that gives participants a corporate-like experience, by working within specialized teams to create a software application in hopes of assisting local businesses in their application development needs.

About the QA team

The Quality Assurance Team is in charge of all documentation regarding the product, running and testing all of the developed applications created by the Research and Development Team, and identifying and reporting errors, ensuring a fully functional product for the client.

About the R&D team

The Research and Development team’s role is to conduct the research necessary to provide a worthy product and use all available tools to develop this product. They are also responsible for developing new and wanted features for the client, and improving and renewing existing products.

Research Team

Our research team focuses on coastal community resilience, working to understand and address environmental and societal challenges facing coastal regions. Through collaboration, data analysis, and community engagement, the team develops insights and solutions that support sustainable and resilient coastal communities.

R&D, QA, and Capstone Teams

The Software Startup Simulator Capstone brings together students in Research & Development, Quality Assurance, and project teams to collaborate on real-world software projects. The R&D team focuses on building and enhancing application features, while QA ensures quality through testing and documentation. Together, these teams simulate a professional development environment, delivering meaningful solutions for real clients.
